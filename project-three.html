The Right Note

Integrated Branding

The Right Note was created for a pro-bono client that was in need of a logo for their new and upcoming music camp. When designing the logo, I also wanted the color to be able to be interchangeable that it could be used in various ways and with different media.

Creating an integrated branding for The Right Note gave the clients a whole new outlook on where they could potentially take their brand.
