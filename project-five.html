Luxury Maids

Signage

Luxury Maids is a business concept that was in need of a logo along with business signage. The purpose of this logo was for the target audience to take one look at the design and know whether or not this business would be dependable and worth the money.

Being luxurious, fancy, impressive, and pricey were key words that I kept in mind throughout the process.
