Hello, You

Corporate Identity System

Hello, You is a card and stationery company in which I designed a complete corporate identity system for, including a letterhead, envelope, and business card. With an elegant, handmade flare, they wanted their audience to feel dainty and pretty while using their products.

The goal was to create a brand that gave a charming experience while also being cheerful and bubbly.
