Vita Bella

Packaging Design

Vita Bella is an Italian chocolate company meaning “Beautiful Life.” The idea of this new design was to step away from the norm of ordinary Italian chocolate packaging.

I wanted to keep the same essence of the company’s history and what the target audience finds important in their everyday life while finding a way to create those images through unique conceptual design.
