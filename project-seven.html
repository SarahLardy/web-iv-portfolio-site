Looking Back, Moving Forward

Book Cover

I had the opportunity to take my own Grandmother’s short stories and make them come to life by formatting her writings and creating its cover. I wanted the cover to help share with the audience an internal look into her life, as well as who she is today. Inspired by her elegance, I designed a cover that both expressed a beautiful grace while also keeping the joyful essence of handwritten letters, which were her original documents that she had given me.

The beginning of the book contains her memoirs and then dives into her individual personal stories to give her memoir more context.
